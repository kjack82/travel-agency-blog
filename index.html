My recent trip to Australia!

The highs and the lows!

After having our initial trip cancelled in 2020 due to the ongoing Covid restrictions, we finally got to jet off on the holiday off a lifetime. However just like the saying "never meet your hero", this trip was a little like that!

My top 5 highlights were as follows;

  1. Rottnest Island
  2. High ropes course in Margaret River
  3. Dolphin cruise
  4. The vibes at Mindarie
  5. The beaches in general, but Hamelin Bay was probably my favourite

    6. The 5 things I did not love;

      • Everywhere was quiet. As a result, it lacked that buzz in most places.
      • It's not an old country, so it lacks history and culture.
      • Food choice was limited. Menus were small, and lacked diversity.
      • Everywhere is so far away as every town in built along the coast.
      • Every car was huge! Not great for the environment
